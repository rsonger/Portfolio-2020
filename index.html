Welcome to the Portolio Website of RobLab

Rob began teaching at Kanazawa Technical College in May 2009 for the purpose of educating Ishikawa's eager young students. Ishikawa children attended Kanazawa Technical College to specialize in an engineering field while also attaining a global mindset and English ability. When Lewis Barksdale joined as President, the school put in motion plans to become the International College of Technology, the first international Kosen in Japan, and continues as suchto this day.
